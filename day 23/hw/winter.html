Winter is a cold season. The weather is usually snowy and windy. People wear warm clothes like coats, hats, and gloves. Days are shorter, and nights are longer. Many enjoy winter activities like building snowmen and drinking hot chocolate.



1. What winter is

Winter is one of the four seasons of the year. It comes after autumn and before spring. During winter, the weather becomes very cold in many places around the world.


2. Weather in winter

In winter, temperatures drop and it often snows or rains. The wind can be strong and cold, and sometimes there is ice on the ground. Days are usually gray and cloudy.


3. Clothes in winter

People wear warm clothes to stay comfortable. This includes thick jackets, sweaters, hats, scarves, gloves, and boots. These help protect the body from the cold.


4. Activities in winter

Many people enjoy winter activities like building snowmen, skiing, sledding, and having snowball fights. At home, people like drinking hot drinks and staying warm inside.


5. Feelings about winter

Some people love winter because it feels magical and peaceful, especially when it snows. Others find it too cold and prefer warmer seasons, but winter is still an important and beautiful time of the year.
