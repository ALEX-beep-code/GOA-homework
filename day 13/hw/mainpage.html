Modern robots are advanced machines that can perform tasks automatically. Today, they are used in many fields such as industry, medicine, and everyday life. For example, robots build products in factories, help doctors in surgeries, and assist people at home. Robots are created using technologies like artificial intelligence (AI) sensors, and machine learning. This allows them to learn, make decisions, and work more independently. In the future, robots will become even smarter and more helpful. They may work closely with humans in many areas, but this also brings questions about safety and jobs. In conclusion, robots are an important part of modern life and will play an even bigger role in the future
