Robots have improved a lot over time. Early robots in the 20th century were simple machines used in factories to do repetitive tasks like assembling cars. In the 1960s, the first industrial robot, Unimate, was introduced and helped automate production. Over the years, robots became smarter and more precise. By the 2000s, they were used not only in factories but also in medicine, space exploration, and everyday life. Today, modern robots use artificial intelligence to learn, adapt, and even interact with people. In the future, robots are expected to become even more advanced, helping humans in areas like healthcare, education, and daily tasks.
