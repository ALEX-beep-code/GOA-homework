Today, robots are much more advanced, intelligent, and versatile. They can:
Work in factories.
Help humans in homes and hospitals.
Explore space and oceans.
Interact with people using AI.

A robot is a machine, especially one programmable via a computer, capable of automatically carrying out a complex series of actions.
A robot can be guided by an external or internal control device. Robots may be humanoid, but most are task-performing machines prioritizing functionality over aesthetics.
