A few years ago, robots became much more advanced and useful in everyday life. Thanks to better technology and artificial intelligence, they could recognize voices, see objects, and make simple decisions. Companies like Boston Dynamics created robots that could walk, run, and even balance like humans or animals. These robots were used in areas like healthcare, delivery services, and customer support. Although they were not fully independent, they showed how quickly robotics was improving and becoming part of daily life.
