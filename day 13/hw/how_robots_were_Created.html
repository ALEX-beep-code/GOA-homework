Robots were created as a result of humans wanting to make work easier and faster. The idea of machines that could act like humans goes back thousands of years, but real robots started to appear in the 20th century. Early inventors combined knowledge from engineering, electricity, and mechanics to build machines that could move and perform simple tasks.

The first humanoid robot was WABOT-1, created in 1973 at Waseda University by Ichiro Kato.

It could walk, use its hands, and interact in a simple way. it was the first robot designed to act

like a human and started the development of modern humanoid robots.
