eyboards are essential input devices used to type text, numbers, and commands into computers and other devices. They usually have keys for letters, numbers, and special functions, including shortcuts and media controls. Keyboards come in different types, such as mechanical, membrane , and wireless, each offering unique feel and responsiveness. They are widely used for work, gaming, and communication, making them a fundamental tool in everyday digital life.

keyboard layout origin

The QWERTY keyboard layout was created for old typewriters to prevent keys from jamming, and it is still used today on most computers.



tips

Some keyboards have special features like backlighting for typing in the dark, and mechanical keys that make a clicking sound and feel more precise.
